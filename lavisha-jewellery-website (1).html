About Lavisha

Home / About Us
Family Owned, Founded On Trust

Timeless Beauty, Thoughtfully Made

Lavisha began as a small family dream — three women, Lalitha, Vasuki and Aishu, who believed that beautiful jewellery shouldn't come with the worry of tarnish, allergies, or a price tag that puts it out of reach.

What started as a handful of pieces shared with friends and family has grown into a curated house of anti-tarnish and stainless steel jewellery, worn daily by women who want quality they can trust and shine that stays.

Every piece we sell is chosen personally, checked for finish and durability, and packaged the way we'd want to receive a gift ourselves. That care is the thread that runs through everything Lavisha makes.
